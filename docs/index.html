GAIA HazLab

GAIA Digital Twin of Soil

A living hydromechanical state of the subsurface — water table depth, soil moisture, and near-surface stiffness — at the ~90 m space and time scales that downstream flood, landslide, and liquefaction models need. Built from high-resolution static layers, assimilated ground sensors and satellites, and a closed water budget, over the Pacific Northwest.
